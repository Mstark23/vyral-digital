FAQ

Common Questions

How does the "try before you buy" model work? +
You sign up with your email. We build your website using AI in under 60 minutes. You get a live preview link to see your actual website. If you love it, you choose a plan and pay. If you don't love it, you walk away - no charge, no obligation. It's completely risk-free.
How long does it actually take? +
Most websites are ready for preview within 30-60 minutes. Complex e-commerce or custom features might take up to 2 hours. You'll receive an email with your preview link as soon as it's ready.
What if I need changes after seeing the preview? +
Every plan includes revision rounds (1-3 depending on your plan). Simply tell us what you'd like changed, and we'll update it. Most revisions are completed within a few hours.
Do I need to pay anything to get started? +
No! The preview is completely free. No credit card required. No deposit. You only pay after you've seen the website and decided you want it.
What do I get after I pay? +
You get all the source files (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), images, and complete documentation. You can host it anywhere you want, or we can help you set it up on your domain. It's 100% yours.
Can you handle e-commerce websites? +
Yes! Our Enterprise plan includes full e-commerce functionality. We can integrate payment processing, product catalogs, shopping carts, and checkout systems. Perfect for online stores.
